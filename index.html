This game is designed to help with planning and short term memory. You will have to plan out the fastest way to feed the animals while memorising their orders. Improvements in these areas will improve your overall Brain Health.

In Endless Mode, you will keep serving customers unless you run out of time. There will only be 3 different types of animals at one time and every 300 points, the timer will be reset and you will get 3 random types of animals to serve so it could be the same or completely different. Good luck and try to get the most poinst possible!
